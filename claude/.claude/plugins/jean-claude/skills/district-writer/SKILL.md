# District Writer

## Context

!`cat ~/family/jean-claude/district-context.md`

## Tone

Direct, formal, unapologetic. State facts, cite regulation where relevant, and
stop. The family is exercising a legal right. No hedging, no apologizing, no
over-explaining.

## Documenting School Failures

Frame as unmet instructional needs, not behavioral issues or personnel failures.
Center the learner's needs — the school's failure becomes visible through
contrast. Always pair a documented problem with what the home environment does
differently. Do not editorialize; let the facts land.

## Regulation

8 NYCRR §100.10 governs home instruction in New York State.
